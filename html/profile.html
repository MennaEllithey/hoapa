FAQ & Help

What is ServiceFinder?

ServiceFinder is a social marketplace connecting people in NZ who need local services with those who offer them. Join now!

What can I get done on ServiceFinder?

Anything that has to do with local NZ services— from repairing vehicles to picking Kiwifruit. ServiceFinder tackles the entire range of services. Get free quotes now.

How to get started?

If you are looking to hire a service, click Get free quotes to post an ad.
If you offer a service, click Find work to list your service and receive free work offers.

What happens after I post my ad?

Sit back and relax. We’ll update you on incoming quotes from service providers. Award the project to your preferred service. There is no obligation to award a project. Post a free ad now.

Can I edit my ad?

Yes, you can edit your ad until you award your project to a service provider. Post a free ad now.

How do I pay for a service?

You pay the service directly. ServiceFinder doesn't mediate in transactions and isn’t involved in the payment process. Payment and all other project details are agreed on between the service provider and the client. ServiceFinder lets people post ads and find work 100% commission-free. Join now!

How to create a great ServiceFinder profile?

Your profile is your ServiceFinder face. Choose a clear username (e.g. Connor) and clear tagline (e.g. Cleaning Expert). Provide a strong description of you or your service. Add photos. Quick, easy, powerful. Join now!
